About MyReviewSite

This site is a site created by a casual gamer who loves to share their thoughts and opinions on games they enjoy. This site is meant to be a fun and interactive space for laidback gamers to find honest reviews and recommendations.

My Mission

The mission of this website is to give other gamers some insight into games they may be interested in playing, with an opinion that is based more on ones possibility of enjoyment rather than a journalistic approach. I want to keep these reviews fair and honest.
